About F1 Portal

F1 Portal is an educational Django website about Formula 1. The website includes categories, teams, drivers, news and season events that are managed through the database.

The project was created as a college laboratory work to demonstrate Django templates, database models, user authentication, ticket orders and password recovery functionality.
